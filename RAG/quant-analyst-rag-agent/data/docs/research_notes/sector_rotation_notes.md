# Sector Rotation Strategy Notes

The sector_rotation_strategy uses sector_relative_strength to tilt toward sectors outperforming the benchmark. The sample backtest from 2020 to 2024 has an annual return of 12.7%, Sharpe of 0.91, max drawdown of -21.2%, and turnover of 64.0%.

## Interpretation

The strategy is plausible but not frictionless. Turnover and transaction costs matter, especially when sector leadership changes quickly during drawdowns or recoveries.
